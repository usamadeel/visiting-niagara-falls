Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions About Our Tours

❓ How far in advance should I book a tour?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance to ensure availability. However, we do accept same-day bookings subject to space availability. Peak season tours (May-September) should be booked 1-2 weeks ahead.

❓ Can children participate in tours?

Absolutely! Most of our tours are family-friendly. Children under 12 often receive discounts, and we offer specially designed Family Adventure tours. Some adventure tours have age/height restrictions for safety — please check specific tour details.

❓ What about weather cancellations or refunds?

We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before your tour for any reason. Tours are occasionally postponed or rescheduled due to severe weather. We'll notify you immediately and reschedule at no charge or provide a full refund.

❓ Do I need a passport for USA-Canada tours?

Yes, a valid passport is required for border crossings between the USA and Canada. NEXUS card holders may use their alternative travel documents. Please verify requirements before booking, as non-US/Canadian citizens may have additional visa requirements.

❓ Are meals included in the tour price?

Most tours include complimentary coffee/snacks or light meals. Our full-day tours typically provide lunch. Fine dining tours (like the Illumination & Dinner Tour) include multi-course meals. Check individual tour details for specifics.
