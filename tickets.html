Got Questions?

Ticket FAQs

How do I receive my tickets?

+
Your tickets are delivered instantly by email as a mobile-friendly PDF or direct to your phone. Simply show the QR code at the attraction — no printing required. You can also access tickets via the link in your confirmation email.

Can I cancel or change my booking?

+
Yes — all tickets come with free cancellation up to 24 hours before the scheduled visit date. Simply contact us by phone or email and we'll process a full refund, no questions asked. Changes to dates can be made up to 48 hours in advance, subject to availability.

Do I need to print my tickets?

+
No printing is required. All attraction tickets are accepted as mobile tickets — just show the QR code on your phone screen at the entrance. We recommend screenshotting your tickets before your visit in case of poor mobile signal near the falls.

Do combo passes cover border crossing?

+
Our Both Sides combo passes include guided transport across the US–Canada border at the Rainbow Bridge. Your guide handles all logistics. You will need a valid passport or NEXUS card. Citizens of most countries can cross for tourism — check visa requirements for your nationality in advance.

Are tickets valid for any date or time-slotted?

+
Most single attraction tickets are date-flexible and valid for any operating day within the season (usually May–November for outdoor attractions, year-round for indoor). Guided tours and combo passes include a specific date and time which can be changed with 48 hours notice. Time-slot restrictions are noted on the ticket page.

Is there a discount for groups?

+
Yes — groups of 10 or more receive additional discounts of 10–15% on top of our already discounted online prices. Contact us directly by phone or email to arrange a group booking and we'll provide a custom quote with the best available pricing and dedicated group logistics.
