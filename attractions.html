Expert Advice

Tips for Visiting

🕗

Arrive Early

Peak-season waits can hit 90 min. Arrive by 8:30AM or book with us to skip the line entirely.

🌧️

Embrace Getting Wet

Ponchos provided at boat rides and Cave of the Winds — wear clothes you don't mind getting soaked.

🎟️

Book Combo Packages

Save up to 35% by bundling 4–8 attractions with hotel pickup in one of our tour packages.

🌙

Visit at Night

The falls illumination runs nightly until midnight — free from viewpoints. Fireworks Fri & Sun in summer.

🇨🇦🇺🇸

See Both Sides

Cave of the Winds for the closest USA thrill. Canadian side for the best panoramic views. You need both!

📅

Best Months

June–Sept for all attractions; Oct for foliage; Dec–Feb for Winter Festival of Lights with fewer crowds.
