Falls Access Attractions

Walk beside, behind, and through the falls with these thrilling up-close experiences.

Cave of the Winds

Descend by elevator to wooden walkways that bring you within feet of the Bridal Veil Falls. Feel the full force of the wind and spray on the Hurricane Deck.

Journey Behind the Falls

Take tunnels carved into the bedrock behind the Horseshoe Falls and emerge on platforms at the base of the cascading water — a truly unique perspective.

A boardwalk along the edge of the Niagara River's Class 6 whitewater rapids — one of the most powerful stretches of river in the world.
